# **App Name**: BizzFly International

## Core Features:

- US Company Formation Info: Display information about US Company Formation services.
- UK Company Formation Info: Show details about UK Company Formation services.
- Payment Gateway Setup Details: Provide details on setting up international payment gateways like Stripe and PayPal.
- Business Bank Account Info: Display information about opening business bank accounts with international platforms like Mercury, Relay, Airwallex, Wise, and Payoneer including international debit cards.
- Tax Filing and Compliance Info: Outline tax filing and compliance support for US and UK companies.
- Contact Form: Contact form for clients.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional blue (#29ABE2) reflecting trust and reliability. 
- Background color: Light grey (#F0F2F5), a desaturated version of the primary color, for a clean, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: A contrasting orange (#FF9933), analogous to the primary hue, to draw attention to calls to action and important information.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines, providing a geometric, fashionable look, and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text, balancing modernity with readability.
- Use modern and clean icons that represent international business, finance, and global connectivity.
- Maintain a clean, well-structured layout with clear sections for each service. Ensure the website is fully responsive across all devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations on page transitions and hover effects to enhance user experience without being distracting.
